Instructions


    Start drawing on the canvas!

    Press Z to undo stroke

    Press X to clear drawing


    App Info

    This app works with a custom Slack bot to allow those who have speech difficulty or those with limited English vocabulary to convey your message through drawings. You can also customize the languages based on your native tongue. Once you finish drawing on the space provided and have selected the word you want to express, we will automatically send the information to the Slack bot and translate it to English and post your desired word in the chat!
